## Payment Assurance Mechanisms

### 1. Immunefi Vaults System

- Projects deposit funds into smart contract vaults
- **Proof-of-assets** visible on-chain
- Payments executed on-chain via dashboard
- KYC required for payout (one-time setup)

**Risk Mitigation**: Only target programs with active vaults

### 2. Challenge Bond System (Our Innovation)

| Element | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| **10% Bond** | Proves we believe in our findings |
| **$500 Minimum** | Prevents spam submissions |
| **30-day Lock** | Allows review period |
| **Slash on Rejection** | Economic truth enforcement |

**Why This Helps Us Get Paid**:
- Programs trust bonded submissions more
- We filter low-confidence findings ourselves
- Higher acceptance rate = better reputation

### 3. Platform Selection Strategy

| Bounty Size | Platform | Why |
|-------------|----------|-----|
| >$50K | Immunefi | Highest payouts, vault system |
| $10K-$50K | Sherlock | Good reputation, fair process |
| <$10K | Code4rena | 100% to auditors, bot races |
