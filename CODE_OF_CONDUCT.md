# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

BIZRA is built on the principle that every human is a node, and every node is a seed. We pledge to make participation in our community a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior includes:**
- Respectful and constructive communication
- Welcoming diverse perspectives
- Graceful acceptance of feedback
- Prioritizing community well-being

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Discriminatory language or conduct
- Any conduct inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated fairly.
